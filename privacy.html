Privacy Policy Effective Date: July 17, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how the Commission Ticket Bot ("the Bot") handles information. Information Collected The Bot processes information provided through Discord interactions, including: - Discord User IDs - Discord Server IDs - Commission request details submitted through forms - Selected commission options and pricing information How Information Is Used The information is used solely to: - Create commission tickets - Calculate commission pricing - Display commission details to the client and staff - Operate the Bot's intended functionality Data Storage The Bot does not permanently store personal information. Commission information exists only while processing requests and within the Discord ticket created for your server. Data Sharing The Bot does not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties. Security Reasonable efforts are made to keep the Bot secure; however, no online service can guarantee absolute security. Changes This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any revisions. Contact For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the Bot developer through Discord.
